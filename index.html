Our Projects

MinDlab

Cloud Driven AR/VR for Smart Home

This perfect MinDLab project aims to achieve could-driven AR/VR technology is integrated into collaboration and immersive experience for users living in the smart home and use the upcoming 5G network technology to process and analysis of spatial data to complete real-time 3D model creation. Users can add or remove any furnitures that scanned by 3D scanner in the VR room. Users can use this application to pre-view the room with furnitures and move around to decorate their homes.

New

1. Users upload depth information of their object view into Could services.

2. Could algorithms will use specific algorithms to process information and split the view to create a lightweight 3D models by converting point-clouds into polygons and record the key elements with its 3D location information.

3. Processing and completing as a complete 3D model from the recorded elements and lightweight 3D models.

4. Real-time display to the users by AR/VR devices.
